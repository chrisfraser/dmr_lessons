DMR Radio Basics

A short, beautiful course on Digital Mobile Radio — built to ground one mission: building Android+DMR radios that bridge voice to a custom PoC platform, on-device and bidirectionally.

Each lesson is self-contained, cites its sources, and ends with a quick self-check. Start at the top, or jump to what you need.
